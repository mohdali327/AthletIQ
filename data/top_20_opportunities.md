## #1 · Haryana Wrestling Akhada Network Digitisation

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Academy |
| **Sport(s)** | Wrestling |
| **Location** | Sonipat, Jhajjar, Hisar, Bhiwani districts — Haryana |
| **AOS** | **8.9** |
| **Timeline** | 🔴 Immediate |

**Why it matters**: Haryana's akhada network is India's most prolific wrestling talent factory — producing Olympic medallists like Bajrang Punia and Vinesh Phogat — yet operates almost entirely offline. Over 400 akhadas across 4 districts train 15,000+ wrestlers with zero digital infrastructure. Digitising this network would create the world's largest grassroots wrestling dataset and establish AthletIQ as the definitive platform for India's strongest Olympic sport.

**Recommended Action**: Deploy WhatsApp bot MVP in 25 pilot akhadas across Sonipat and Jhajjar within 4 weeks. Collect athlete profiles (name, age, weight class, competition history) and training attendance. Partner with Haryana Wrestling Association for legitimacy and scale.
