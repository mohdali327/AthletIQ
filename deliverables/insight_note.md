# AthletIQ — Executive Insight Note

### India's Grassroots-to-Podium Sports Intelligence Platform

---

## The Landscape

India's sports ecosystem has a **₹10,000-crore blind spot** at the grassroots level:

- **1.4 billion people, ~215 Olympic athletes.** India's athlete-to-population conversion rate is the lowest among the world's top-20 economies — 80× worse than Australia, 40× worse than Kenya. The talent exists. The pathways don't.

- **7 states produce 85% of India's medal-sport athletes.** Haryana, Punjab, Manipur, Tamil Nadu, Jharkhand, Odisha, and Karnataka dominate — leaving 21 states and 600+ districts with near-zero formal talent identification. Millions of potential champions remain invisible.

- **Less than 3% of grassroots athletes** have access to structured coaching data, performance tracking, or systematic progression pathways. India invests ₹2,700 crore/year in sports (SAI + Khelo India) but has no unified system to measure where the money goes or whom it reaches.

---

## The Opportunity

**AthletIQ is building India's missing sports data layer** — connecting every village akhada, rural tournament, school competition, and grassroots academy into a single intelligence platform. With the **Commonwealth Games 2036** potentially on Indian soil, there is a **10-year window** to build the data infrastructure that transforms India's sports output from 60 Olympic medals (all-time) to 60 medals in a *single cycle*.

## 3 Immediate Actions

1. **Launch Haryana Wrestling Pilot** *(Month 1)*: Digitise 25 akhadas in Sonipat-Jhajjar using WhatsApp bot + FitEvents OS. Create India's first grassroots wrestling dataset. Proof of concept for state government MOU.

2. **Close 3 CSR Partnerships** *(Month 1–3)*: Target JSW Foundation, Tata Trusts, and one banking-sector foundation. Pitch: *"Measure the impact of every rupee spent on grassroots sports — from village discovery to national selection."* Lead with Medal Mission 2036 narrative.

3. **Deploy Computer Vision Prototype** *(Month 3–4)*: Phone-camera biomechanical analysis for wrestling stance and sprint form. Zero-hardware talent screening tool. Demonstrate at Tamil Nadu State Athletics Championship for media visibility and federation buy-in.
